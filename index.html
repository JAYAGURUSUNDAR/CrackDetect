Explanation

The railway track crack simulation illustrates the impact of stress and fatigue on the structural integrity of railway tracks. Over time, small imperfections can develop into larger cracks, which, if unchecked, can lead to significant safety hazards.

The graph shows the relationship between time and crack length, providing insight into the crack propagation speed and potential intervention points for maintenance and repair.
